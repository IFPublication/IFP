What is IF Medical?

IF Medical is a branch of IF Publication dedicated to amplifying voices in medicine, healthcare, and public health. It’s a collaborative space for students, professionals, patients, and advocates to share insights, challenge systems, and imagine a more just and connected medical future.

Who Can Contribute?

  • Premed, PA, nursing, medical, and allied health students
  • Physicians, nurses, EMTs, and other clinical professionals
  • Public health experts and educators
  • Patients, caregivers, and community health workers
  • Medical artists and science communicators
  • Anyone passionate about medicine and care

What You Can Publish

  • Medical reflections and personal narratives
  • Original research summaries and case discussions
  • Creative writing: stories, poetry, essays
  • Visual medicine: sketches, infographics, and diagrams
  • Clinical experiences and first-hand insights
  • Public health critiques, op-eds, and data stories
  • MCAT tips, med school survival guides, or anatomy tricks
  • Letters to the healthcare system or advocacy pieces

How It Works

  1. Submit your work using guided templates or direct upload
  2. Engage with others through comments, feedback, and forums
  3. Get featured on IF Medical—searchable and tagged by topic

Goals of IF Medical

  • Make medical knowledge accessible to all
  • Center stories often excluded from traditional spaces
  • Promote cross-disciplinary collaboration
  • Challenge injustice and foster compassionate care
  • Create a living archive of medical thought and creativity
