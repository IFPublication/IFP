What is IF Medical?

IF Medical is a branch of IF Publication dedicated to publishing thought-provoking, evidence-based, and creative content related to medicine, healthcare, public health, and clinical innovation. Whether you’re a student, healthcare worker, researcher, patient advocate, or curious reader, IF Medical is your space to write, think, question, and collaborate on all things medical.

Who Can Contribute?

  • Premedical & medical students
  • Public health professionals
  • Physicians & nurses
  • EMTs, PAs, therapists, and allied health workers
  • Patients, caregivers, & advocates
  • Anyone passionate about medicine

What You Can Publish

  • Original research summaries (no journal needed!)
  • Medical reflections or patient narratives
  • Creative nonfiction or storytelling from clinical work
  • Ethical dilemmas or policy proposals
  • Visual medicine: drawings, infographics, or case sketches
  • Guides, lesson plans, or tutorials (like anatomy study tips, MCAT strategies, med school survival guides, etc.)
  • Personal experiences: first code blue, being a caregiver, working in underserved areas
  • Public health op-eds or community health analyses
  • Letters to the system: challenge injustice, advocate for change

How It Works

  1. Submit your work via guided templates or upload it directly
  2. Join community discussions, respond to others, and form collaborations
  3. Your work becomes visible on the IF Medical page, tagged and searchable

Goals of IF Medical

  • Democratize medical writing and reflection
  • Give pre-health and healthcare workers a voice
  • Encourage collaboration across fields and regions
  • Promote health equity, critical thinking, and compassion
  • Build a living archive of stories and resources to inspire change
