What is IF Life?

IF Life is a branch of IF Publication devoted to stories, ideas, and reflections about real life — identity, relationships, spirituality, resilience, self-discovery, and the big questions that shape who we are. It’s a place for both honesty and imagination.

Learn More

Who Can Contribute?

  • Writers, thinkers, and creatives
  • Students and professionals
  • Parents, partners, and caregivers
  • People exploring identity, healing, or transformation
  • Anyone with a story to tell

What You Can Publish

  • Personal essays and memoir
  • Spiritual reflections or philosophical thoughts
  • Creative nonfiction, fiction, and poetry
  • Identity journeys (race, gender, culture, disability, etc.)
  • Love stories, grief letters, forgiveness pieces
  • Coming-of-age narratives
  • Experiments in voice, perspective, or storytelling
  • Open letters, manifestos, or intimate reflections

How It Works

  1. Submit your story through templates or upload directly
  2. Engage with others through comments and community
  3. Be featured on IF Life with tags and visibility

Goals of IF Life

  • Create a welcoming space for real human experience
  • Celebrate diverse voices and inner lives
  • Offer healing, humor, and hope through storytelling
  • Bridge culture, emotion, and meaning
  • Encourage creativity, honesty, and self-reflection
