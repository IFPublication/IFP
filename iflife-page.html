What is IF Life?

IF Life is a branch of IF Publication devoted to stories and reflections about what it means to live, feel, grow, and become. It’s a space for raw honesty, deep imagination, and the voices that shape personal and collective experience—across identity, relationships, culture, spirituality, and more.

Who Can Contribute?

  • Writers, thinkers, and creatives
  • Students, professionals, and lifelong learners
  • Parents, partners, caregivers, and survivors
  • People exploring identity, healing, or change
  • Anyone with a story to tell or a truth to share

What You Can Publish

  • Personal essays, memoir, and reflections
  • Spiritual meditations or philosophical thoughts
  • Creative nonfiction, fiction, or poetry
  • Identity journeys (race, gender, disability, heritage, etc.)
  • Love stories, grief letters, and forgiveness pieces
  • Coming-of-age or life-transition narratives
  • Letters to the past, future, or self
  • Open letters, manifestos, or experimental writing

How It Works

  1. Submit your story using guided templates or direct upload
  2. Engage in discussion, leave comments, and build community
  3. Your story becomes part of IF Life—searchable, tagged, and shared

Goals of IF Life

  • Celebrate real human experience in all its complexity
  • Uplift diverse voices and lived truths
  • Create a space for healing, connection, and courage
  • Bridge storytelling with culture, emotion, and meaning
  • Encourage creative self-expression and honest reflection
