IF Justice: Narrative Pathology Casebook

Role: Research Assistant & Case Developer

Project Type: Interactive Storytelling & Research Archive

Description:

This project explores the intersections of forensic pathology, Indigenous health sovereignty, and medical ethics through narrative case studies. Each entry highlights a real or fictionalized case to prompt public discussion and professional reflection.

Sample Entry

“The Unclaimed Bones” – a story following an M.E. intern’s discovery about identity misclassification on a reservation border.
