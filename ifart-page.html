What is IF Art?

IF Art is a branch of IF Publication that showcases creative expression in all its forms. From painting to poetry, from digital media to performance—this space is for artists, writers, and creators to explore what art can do, say, heal, question, and build.

Who Can Contribute?

  • Artists, designers, and photographers
  • Writers, poets, and storytellers
  • Performers and experimental creators
  • Students and first-time contributors
  • Anyone with something creative to share

What You Can Publish

  • Photography, digital art, illustration, collage
  • Poetry, flash fiction, and essays
  • Videos of performances, dance, or spoken word
  • Comics, zines, and multimedia projects
  • Art about identity, politics, or community
  • Letters, dreams, unfinished experiments
  • Behind-the-scenes process or artist statements

How It Works

  1. Submit your work through templates or file uploads
  2. Include your title, tags, and any notes or meaning
  3. Your work gets featured on IF Art with credit and visibility

Goals of IF Art

  • Celebrate the diversity of human creativity
  • Make space for emotional, political, and abstract expression
  • Encourage vulnerability, storytelling, and collaboration
  • Uplift emerging and underrepresented artists
