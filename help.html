Need assistance navigating IF Publication? Find answers to common questions, publishing tips, and ways to get support below.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking the “Login” button at the top right corner and selecting “Sign Up.”

Is it free to publish on IF?

Yes! Publishing, reading, and collaborating on IF Publication is completely free.

Can I edit or delete my content?

Yes. Once logged in, go to your dashboard to manage, edit, or delete any of your published work.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Please use the "Report" button on any post or email us directly at Isabella.L.Ford@ifpublication.com.
